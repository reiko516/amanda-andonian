First of all, thanks again for joining us at Business-Software.com! We couldn't grow the way we have if not for your presence on the site, and we have some exciting announcements regarding Business-Software.com and how you can contribute.


New Login Interface

Starting today, you'll no longer be signing into Business-Software.com through WordPress. Instead, you'll log into the interface via the "Login" link at the top of the Business-Software.com homepage.
Once you're signed in, you'll see your name at the upper right-hand corner of the page where you can access Quick Links to edit and view your public profile, adjust your profile settings add new blog posts, and answer user questions in the Business-Software.com Community! No matter where you are on the site, you can access your account from here.

Easily Manage Your Public Profile and Blog Posts

Updating your profile and submitting new posts has never been easier! Now you can access all of your public profile information, as well as your blog posts, from your Contributor account page.

Show Your Expertise in the Community

We've completely revamped the Business-Software.com Community to make it easier for you to answer user questions, up-vote answers that you like, and even ask your own software-related questions! 

Answering questions is not only a great way to show your expertise, but we hope to continue to grow the Community into a place where Business-Software.com can connect you with potential customers or clients who might be interested in the software and/or services that you have to offer. More on that to come!
